About The Project

This project was developed for the course of Probabilistic Graphical Models of Federal Institute of Education, Science and Technology of Ceará - IFCE. It uses Hidden Markov Models to classify a sentence in POS Tags. n corpus linguistics, part-of-speech tagging (POS tagging or PoS tagging or POST), also called grammatical tagging or word-category disambiguation, is the process of marking up a word in a text (corpus) as corresponding to a particular part of speech, based on both its definition and its context—i.e., its relationship with adjacent and related words in a phrase, sentence, or paragraph. A simplified form of this is commonly taught to school-age children, in the identification of words as nouns, verbs, adjectives, adverbs, etc.
